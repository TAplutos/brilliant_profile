Divisor Sigma Function

Calculus Level 5

If

S=∑p∑n=2∞τ(n)−1pnS=\displaystyle \sum_{p} \displaystyle \sum_{n=2}^{\infty} \dfrac{\tau (n)-1}{p^n}S=p∑​n=2∑∞​pnτ(n)−1​

where p∈Np\in\mathbb{N}p∈N is greater than 1 and is NOT a perfect power.

Find ⌈10000S⌉\lceil 10000S \rceil⌈10000S⌉

Notes:

  • τ(x)\tau (x)τ(x) is the divisor sigma function. This represents the number of factors of a number including 1 and itself. For example, τ(6)=σ0(6)=4\tau(6)=\sigma_0(6)=4τ(6)=σ0​(6)=4.

  • ⌈⋅⌉ \lceil \cdot \rceil ⌈⋅⌉ denotes the ceiling function.


The answer is 10000.
