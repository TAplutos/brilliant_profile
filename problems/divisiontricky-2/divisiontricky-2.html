We have (a+1)(a+2)(a+3)≡b(moda)(a+1)(a+2)(a+3)\equiv b (mod a)(a+1)(a+2)(a+3)≡b(moda). Upon expansion, every term on the LHS is clearly will be divisible by a. Thus we only need the last term: 6. And that is our remainder.
