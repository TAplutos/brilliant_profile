From the given polynomial, we have

∑ri=3\sum r_i = 3∑ri​=3

and

∑ri−1=0.\sum r_i^{-1} = 0.∑ri−1​=0.

The sum of the reciprocals of the roots come from the fact that the polynomial with reciprocal roots has its coefficients reversed.

x5−3x4−1=0x4(x−3)=1x4=(x−3)−1x−9=(x−3)2x∑ri−9=∑(ri−3)2ri=∑ri2−6ri+9ri=∑ri−6+9ri−1=∑ri−∑6+9∑ri−1=3−5(6)+0=−27. \begin{aligned} x^5 - 3x^4 - 1 &= 0 \\ x^4(x - 3) &= 1 \\ x^4 &= (x-3)^{-1} \\ x^{-9} &= \frac{(x-3)^2}{x} \\ \sum r_i^{-9} &= \sum \frac{(r_i - 3)^2}{r_i} \\ &= \sum \frac{r_i^2 - 6r_i + 9}{r_i} \\ &= \sum r_i - 6 + 9r_i^{-1} \\ &= \sum r_i - \sum 6 + 9 \sum r_i^{-1} \\ &= 3 - 5(6) + 0 \\ &= \boxed{-27}. \end{aligned} x5−3x4−1x4(x−3)x4x−9∑ri−9​​=0=1=(x−3)−1=x(x−3)2​=∑ri​(ri​−3)2​=∑ri​ri2​−6ri​+9​=∑ri​−6+9ri−1​=∑ri​−∑6+9∑ri−1​=3−5(6)+0=−27​.​
