Now, for this to be represented in a+b\sqrt a+\sqrt ba​+b​, we need to find (a+b)2=a+b+2ab(\sqrt a+\sqrt b)^2=a+b+2\sqrt{ab}(a​+b​)2=a+b+2ab​. Since a,b are integers, a must be equal to 1 or 2 while b is equal to either 4 or 3. Checking 2 and 3: (2+3)2=2+26+3⇒5+26(\sqrt2+\sqrt3)^2=2+2\sqrt6+3\Rightarrow 5+2\sqrt6(2​+3​)2=2+26​+3⇒5+26​
