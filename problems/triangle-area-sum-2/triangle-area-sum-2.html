Triangle Area Sum 2

Geometry Level 5

Find all possible areas of a triangle with 222 sides of lengths 111111 and 121212 and one 60∘60^{\circ}60∘ angle.

If the sum of the areas of the 333 smallest triangles is ab,a\sqrt{b},ab​, where aaa and bbb are integers and bbb is square-free, enter your answer as a+ba+ba+b.

  • Useful Brilliant wiki: Area of a Triangle
