Geometry Kings, Solve This!

Geometry Level 5

Let the excircle of the triangle ABCABCABC lying opposite to AAA touch its side BCBCBC at the point A1A_{1}A1​. Define the points B1B_{1}B1​ and C1C_{1}C1​ analogously. Suppose that the circumcentre of the triangle A1B1C1A_{1}B_{1}C_{1}A1​B1​C1​ lies on the circumcircle of the triangle ABCABCABC. Find the measure of the largest angle in triangle ABCABCABC in degrees.


The answer is 90.

by Department 8, 27, Israel
