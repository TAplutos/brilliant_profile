783 Followers Problem

Number Theory Level 2

a(a+b)(a+b+c)(a+b+c+d)=783a(a+b)(a+b+c)(a+b+c+d)=783a(a+b)(a+b+c)(a+b+c+d)=783

Given that a,b,c,da,b,c,da,b,c,d are positive integers and that they satisfy the above equation, find their product: a×b×c×da \times b \times c \times d a×b×c×d.

1⋅3⋅9⋅291\cdot 3 \cdot 9\cdot 291⋅3⋅9⋅29

Thus we have

1=a3=a+b9=a+b+c29=a+b+c+d \begin{aligned} 1&=a\\ 3&=a+b\\ 9&=a+b+c\\ 29&=a+b+c+d\\ \end{aligned} 13929​=a=a+b=a+b+c=a+b+c+d​
