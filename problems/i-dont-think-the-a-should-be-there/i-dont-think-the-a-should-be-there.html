It's not quite enough to say that ln⁡(a−1) \ln(a-1) ln(a−1) and ln⁡(a−2) \ln(a-2) ln(a−2) are undefined. You need that one of the terms of the sum is undefined, since the log properties you use assume that each log is separately defined. (E.g. ln⁡(−2⋅−3) \ln(-2 \cdot -3) ln(−2⋅−3) is defined even though ln⁡(−2) \ln(-2) ln(−2) and ln⁡(−3) \ln(-3) ln(−3) are undefined.)
