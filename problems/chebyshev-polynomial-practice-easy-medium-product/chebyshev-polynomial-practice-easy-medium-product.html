Chebyshev Polynomial Practice: Product

Algebra Level 3

Evaluate

cos⁡(10∘)cos⁡(20∘)cos⁡(30∘)cos⁡(40∘)cos⁡(50∘)cos⁡(60∘)cos⁡(70∘)cos⁡(80∘)\cos(10^{\circ})\cos(20^{\circ})\cos(30^{\circ})\cos(40^{\circ})\cos(50^{\circ})\cos(60^{\circ})\cos(70^{\circ})\cos(80^{\circ})cos(10∘)cos(20∘)cos(30∘)cos(40∘)cos(50∘)cos(60∘)cos(70∘)cos(80∘)

If the answer can be expressed as ab\dfrac{a}{b}ba​

Find a+ba+ba+b


The answer is 259.

Break up the equation into 3 parts.

(cos⁡(10)cos⁡(50)cos⁡(70))⋅(cos⁡(20)cos⁡(40)cos⁡(80))⋅(cos⁡(30)cos⁡(60))(\cos(10)\cos(50)\cos(70))\cdot(\cos(20)\cos(40)\cos(80))\cdot(\cos(30)\cos(60))(cos(10)cos(50)cos(70))⋅(cos(20)cos(40)cos(80))⋅(cos(30)cos(60))

cos⁡(10)cos⁡(110)cos⁡(130)\cos(10)\cos(110)\cos(130)cos(10)cos(110)cos(130). Now we are looking at

T3(cos⁡(x))−32=4x3−3x−32=0T_3(\cos(x))-\frac{\sqrt3}{2}=4x^3-3x-\frac{\sqrt3}{2}=0T3​(cos(x))−23​​=4x3−3x−23​​=0

Focusing on the second set

cos⁡(20)cos⁡(100)cos⁡(140)\cos(20)\cos(100)\cos(140)cos(20)cos(100)cos(140). We have
