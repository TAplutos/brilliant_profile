Synthetic Geometry Group

Latest details.

Xuming Liang would like to start up a Synthetic Geometry group on Brilliant. The focus would be on understanding how to approach Geometry proofs that you would see on an Olympiad, as opposed to simply calculating the values through brute force.

The details are not fully set yet, but we will most likely meet up once a week on Slack, cover a particular topic, and then post notes/wikis that summarize what we have learnt.

If this interests you, please send me a message.

When posting on Brilliant:

  • Use the emojis to react to an explanation, whether you're congratulating a job well done , or just really confused .

  • Try to contribute something new to the discussion, whether it is an extension, generalization or other idea related to the challenge.
  • Stay on topic — we're all here to learn more about math and science, not to hear about your favorite get-rich-quick scheme or current world events.
